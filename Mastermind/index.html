Interactive Demonstration

To use this, think of a combination of numbers between 1-6 (ie 1244), The computer will make a guess and ask for a response. Use the mastermind peg system - black = right number in the right place, white = right number in the wrong place to rate the guess. Leave it blank if there are no pegs to be used.

Example:
Your number - 1223
Computer's guess - 4223
Response - BB
(two 2's in the right place)
